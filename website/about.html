About

The ICFP Programming Contest is a yearly competition open to all. The 2021 edition is organised by Alex Lang and Jasper Van der Jeugt. Its results will be announced at the 2021 ICFP conference.

For those interested in learning more, Wikipedia provides additional details about the contest and its history.
